## Abstract

The music of two tabs open at once, neon underpaint bleeding through the surface, an exposed wall nobody bothered to cover. What if opposites aren't kept apart, but folded into one another until the seam itself becomes the subject? The work stacks rather than resolves, piling the lush onto the wrong and trusting the friction to hold.

The result is a chimera, an image built from titillating pairings: heroin and cheesecake, the sweet pressed flat against the sour until neither can be parsed alone. This is the side of excess over editing where each piece arrives as a single creature stitched from incompatible appetites. Not a contradiction to be solved, but one to be savored.

## Artists

### [pskl](https://nft.pascal.cc)

is a Berlin-based artist focused on the mutations caused by automation of the creative process. By encoding art direction into autonomous virtual machines, their output passively repurposes internet material into new results. Striving to summon a subjective soul into the machine realm, they also explore new methods of production that have inherent properties of scalability. The modular aspects of their practice enable us to gaze into an imminent future where the notion of art production is constantly redefined: a scaled body of work striving to embrace noise, reconsidering every single error and side effect as a fundamental benefit.
